Accreditation

The School of Business at Howard University was the first business school in Washington, DC to be accredited by the Association of Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). Holding such as accreditation assures the quality of instruction to prospects, graduates and employers. This accreditation is earned through a rigorous accreditation process; for that reason, there are only 681 institutions worldwide accredited by the association.

For more information about AACSB visit www.aacsb.edu

Howard University is regionally accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education (MSCHE). Regionally accresdited universities afford greater opportunities for graduates who which to pursue further education. MSCHE accreditation further confirms Howard University's commitment to excellence for which the university has long been recognized. For more information on MSCHE accreditation visit www.msche.org.
