About Howard University's Online Executive MBA


The Online EMBA program is tailored to respond to the needs of experienced private and public sector proffesionals with the competitive edge needed for high-level leadership positions across the world's most dynamic companies and the federal government

Delivered as a flexible online program, the Howard EMBA cam be completed in as few as 18 months, allowing students to pursue their degree while maintaining work and family obligations.

During the program you will learn from and network with the same acclaimed faculty that teaches our on-campus business programs. The EMBA curriculum addresses the topics of finance, management, accounting, marketing, statistics, communication, ethics, entrepreneurship, and strategy all from the perspective of executive leadership.
