Dr. Lim is an Assistant Professor in Accounting. She has taught both the traditional face to face class and the online class. Her research interests are in auditing, financial market, and minorities in the accounting field. She has her bachelor degree from Universitas Airlangga, an MBA and Ph.D. from the George Washington University.
