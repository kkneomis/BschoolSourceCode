Biography

Dr. Subodh Kulkarni received his M.S and Ph.D. in Industrial Management from Clemson University. He also holds a bachelor’s and master’s degree in Pharmacy from the Institute of Chemical Technology, University of Mumbai (MUICT) India. His current research interests include different areas related to strategic management, such as stakeholder management and ethics, dynamic capabilities, Amartya Sen’s Capabilities Approach, international business, transaction cost economics, supply chain management, and governance. His research has appeared in a number of refereed journals, such as Human Relations, Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Business and Economic Studies, and the Journal of Supply Chain Management, among others. He has also served as a reviewer for many scholarly journals. He currently serves on the editorial board of Current Issues of Business & Law.

Dr. Kulkarni has made numerous presentations at the annual conferences of the Academy of Management, Society for Advancement of Management, INFORMS, Decision Science Institute, and many others. He has also worked on several research grants from the National Science Foundation, Lucent Technologies Foundation, NASA, and the Kauffman Foundation, among others.
