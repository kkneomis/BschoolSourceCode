Dr. Guang Yang is an Assistant Professor of Marketing at the Department of Marketing, School of Business, Howard University. He holds a B.S. in Management Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology, a Master of Economics from Dongbei University of Finance and Economics, both in China, and a Ph.D. in Marketing from the University of California, Irvine. Before joining Howard University in 2009, Dr. Yang had been with SUNY Oneonta, Bryant University, Dalian University of Technology, and The George Washington University. He has published in “Journal of Product Innovation Management”, “Journal of International Business Studies”, “Innovative Marketing”, “International Journal of China Marketing”, and “International Journal of Marketing & Business Communication”.
