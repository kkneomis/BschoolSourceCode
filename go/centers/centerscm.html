Center for Excellence in Supply Chain Management

The Center for Excellence in Supply Chain Management (CESCM) at Howard University is a part of the School of Businessâ€™ Masters and undergraduate programs. We strive to enhance the academic experience of our students by exposing them to the business community and getting them involved with corporate America and Supply Chain Management from the onset of their academic experience. Members of the Advisory Board and other corporate partners provide Supply Chain Management (SCM) students with internship opportunities, class lecturers, corporate office experience, SCM conferences, scholarships and curriculum input. This collaboration between our students and the business world enables our SCM students to be amply prepared with practical experience for careers in Supply Chain Management and to understand the breadth of the field. As a result of our relationship with the corporate sector, we are able to work in tandem with the needs of the business community and provide the best education for state-of-the-art SCM students. Supply Chain Management is an exciting discipline which touches upon all aspects of the business.
