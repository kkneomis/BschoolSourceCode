Become a Corporate Sponsor

Reach a diverse group of talented young professionals early. As the best and brightest students are identified and secured by companies earlier and earlier, this program allows you to make your case and ensure freshman and sophomore students know about the opportunities and benefits of your firm before they are already locked in by competitor offers in their junior and senior years. Your staff will work directly with a designated student team, building relationships with talented students who will ensure your corporate on-campus presence is apparent through “company days,” in which each month team members wear branded materials, and inclusion in program materials and displays.

As a sponsor, you will also receive a designated staff liaison to help development and implement your campus outreach strategy. This will include priority scheduling of on-campus recruitment events, added event promotions, and the option to make presentations to designated classes.
