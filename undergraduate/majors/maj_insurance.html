Finance - Risk Management & Insurance, and Actuarial Science

The Department of Finance and International Business has now two separate concentrations: Risk Management & insurance and Actuarial Science under Finance Major. The insurance program at Howard University started in 1979 as the ONLY Historically Black College and Universities (HBCU), and is still the only program in Washington DC and Maryland area. Currently, Howardâ€™s Risk Management and Insurance program is recognized as the only insurance program among HBCU (source: American Risk and Insurance Association, www.aria.org) and only HBCU school offering actuarial science curriculum acknowledged by Society of Actuary (www.soa.org) and Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS). Our actuarial science program provides approved courses for VEE (validation by educational experience, www.soa.org) for the actuarial exams. Furthermore, our program is the first and only active HBCU in the country who is a member of Gamma Iota Sigma, the International Insurance Fraternity organization. This program is closely affiliated with The Center for Insurance Education (CIE) that provides summer internship school scholarships, internships, and assistance in professional examination preparation.
