Biography

Dr. Mobolurin obtained a Ph.D. in Operations Research and Industrial Engineering from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst in 1983, where he also served as a Research and Teaching Associate. He has worked as a transportation systems analyst and consultant. He has conducted training sessions on “Corporate Applications of MIS”, for a USAID sponsored Executive Development Program. His current interests are in the area of application of Information Technology in decision-making, expert systems, group decision support systems and quality management.

Dr. Mobolurin teaches Programming courses (Java, Visual C++.NET, C++), Information Systems Consulting, in the School of Business. He has taught courses in Expert Systems, Operations Research, Management Science, Statistics, Project Management, and Database Development. He joined the School of Business as an Assistant Professor in 1983and is currently an Associate Professor. He served as the Acting Chairman for the Department of Information Systems and Analysis from 1991 till 1993, and served as the Chairman till the 2000.

He has worked as a Transportation Systems Analyst and Consultant and worked in Faculty Corporate Exchanges and Fellowships with Corning Glass Works, Eli Lilly, and Boeing Corporation

He is a member of INFORMS, American Statistical Association, the Association for Computing Machinery.
