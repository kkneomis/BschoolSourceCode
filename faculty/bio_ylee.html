Dr. Youngho Lee, Chair joined the Department of Finance, International Business and Insurance joined in 1986, after serving as Assistant Professor at Catholic University of America and George Washington University. His primary research areas of interest include: Fundamental Analysis of Stock Returns and Emerging market Capital Development. He has taught: Corporate Finance, Investments & Portfolio Management, International Business, and International Financial Management. Lee was inducted as Honorary Golden Key Society Member, Howard University Chapter, “Outstanding Professor for Dedication, Excellence and Service”, by the Graduate Business Students Association, Howard University, and inducted as a member of Beta Gamma Sigma by the George Washington University Chapter. He is also an interpreter in English and Korean languages for numerous conferences and seminars and a semi-professional soloist at a local church. Lee holds a DBA from the George Washington University, and MBA and BA from Seoul National University.
